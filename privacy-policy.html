Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

At Free Fire Nepal Tournament, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as name, email address, and phone number when you register for the tournament.
  • Payment details for registration and prize distribution purposes.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device information for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage tournament registrations and communication.
  • To process payments and distribute prizes.
  • To improve our website and services through analytics.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers to process payments or manage tournament logistics.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • You have the right to request corrections or deletions of your personal information.
